About Reana Kirzten Villanueva

Known for showcasing her performance in vocals and piano since her early childhood, along with being formerly interpreted by her peers under the producer alias, Elektra.ck, Reana Kirzten Villanueva (Artist Name : Reana Kirzten) is a NYC-born, well-rounded musician, multimedia creative, and producer who specializes in electronic, pop, alternative, and soundtrack cinematic music. As an experimental artist, Reana conveys an openness to a wide variety of music styles and genres. Reana also publicizes her craft through visual content, such as video editing and filming informative commentary videos.

Interests

Reana's interests include singing, producing original songs and arrangements, along with listening to music with a sound design analysis mindset. Reana has a strong fondness towards the setting where music and technology connect, and is fascinated by the development of sound within a song. Outside of music, she enjoys writing, biking, taking city walks, fashion, and videography.

Ambitions

Reana uses her experience, creativity, and desire to collaborate with others to help her succeed with her long-term ambitions, hoping to land a career as a professional audio engineer, sound designer, or service-oriented music producer for global multimedia corporations and cinematography/film projects, acquire creative entrepreneurship knowledge to start her own music production recording business, as well as release original music on Spotify as an independent artist on the side. Reana aspires to create an impact on others by encouraging them to make the most out of their artistry and use innovation to strengthen their resolves.
